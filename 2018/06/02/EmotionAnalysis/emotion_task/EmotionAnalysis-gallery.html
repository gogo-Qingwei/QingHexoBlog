EMOTION ANALYSIS ON CUSTOMER REVIEWS

Sentiment Analysis · Machine Learning · Python · D3.js

In Brief

Companies care about customer attitudes to their server. Nowadays, the online stores like Amazon.com and social networking websites provide large enough reviews from customers, or potential customers for analysis. It costs much less to grasp interesting information from internet and build models on those data. Methods like Sentiment Analysis, Networking Analysis are popular ways to realizing emotion conveyed by customers' voice.

Emotion Analysis I used here is for selecting special nouns which are corresponding to 8 types of emotions: Anger, Anticipation, Disgust, Fear, Joy, Sadness, Surprise and Trust. In another word, we want to get 8 lists of nouns for all emotion types, each of the lists refers to the frequencies of nouns in reviews. the reviews are wrote for one diabetes product sale at Amazon.
